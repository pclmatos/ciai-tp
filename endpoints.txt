GET /apartments - Retrieves all apartment
POST /apartments - Adds a new apartment with the information in the payload
POST /apartments/{id}/bid - Bid on an apartment with {id} with period information in the payload
GET /apartments/{id} - Retrieves the information of apartment with {id}
GET /apartments/{id}/bids - List all bids on apartment with {id}
GET /apartments?location=<location> - Retrieves all apartments located in <location>
DELETE /apartments/{id} - Removes apartment with {id}

POST /person - Adds a new person with information in the payload
GET /person
GET /person/{id}
GET /person/bids - Retrieves the bid of the logged in person
DELETE /person - Removes person logged in to the session
POST /person/checkin?apartment=<id> - The current person checks-in to apartment with <id>
POST /person/checkout?apartment=<id> - The current person checks-out from apartment with <id>


PUT /manager?apartment=<id>&bid=<bid> - Decides the state of bid with {bid} for apartment with {id} with the information in the payload
